Burned apartment

Someone lit a fire in Ben's apartment last night. His notes on the cult are gone, turned to ash. A second floor window was broken and there is blood on the table. A trail of ashen footprints leads to the bedroom. Was this a simple mistake, a drunken accident, or something more sinister?

×
Puncture wounds

Cass woke up in Ben's apartment after a significant fire with two puncture wounds just above her collarbone. A double syringe of poison? A vampire bite? An overly-enthusiastic doctor? Who's to know? In a dream of a starry sea, they emitted golden light.

×
A dream of a starry sea

Cass passed out part way through investigating Ben's burned apartment and dreamt of an impossibly clear ocean above a starry void and below a shining aurora. A cityscape glowed in the distance. A small green fish attacked her. The wounds carried into the waking world.

A fish?

A small round green fish with yellow eyes, too many teeth, and a pulsing blue light in its forehead. Seen in dreams of a starry sea. Fast and aggressive.

Ashen body

The outline of a man, formed entirely of a fine white ash. Found in Ben's apartment on the bed after a fire. The ash doesn't match the ash found anywhere else in the apartment.

Fabric scraps

A torn scrap of fabric, unusually heavy and exceedingly black. Found in Ben's apartment with the ashen body.

Broken syringes

A handful of small glass syringes in various states of damage. Some still contain a light red residue. Found in Ben's apartment with the ashen body.
